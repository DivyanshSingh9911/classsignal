Newton's Second Law — the bigger the force, the faster something accelerates!
F = m × a
• F = Force (Newtons)
• m = Mass (kg)
• a = Acceleration (m/s²)

Think of pushing a shopping cart 🛒 — push harder (↑F), it speeds up faster. Heavier cart (↑m), harder to accelerate!
